CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • Ibarra is ayoung, wealthy Mestizo who has recently returned to the Philippines from seven years of study in Europe.
  • He is smart, well-respeted, and very idealistic.
    		•
    Maria Clara de los Santos y Alba
  • She is a woman of high social status and is believed to be Father Damaso's goddaughter as well as the daughter of Kapitan Tiago.
  • She is truly Father Damaso's biological child, the result of their scandalous relationship with Kapitan Tiago's wife.
    		•
    Father Damaso
  • Father Damaso is an elderly, ruthlessly corrupt Spanish priest who has spent almost two decades residing among the native Filipinos.
  • He has spent all that time among them, but the years have done nothing to endear him or give any sympathy to his "flock."
  • He is profoundly prejudiced, spiteful, and vengeful, and has no problem using his immune influence to destroy people who have wronged him.
    		•
    Capitan Tiago
  • Capitan Tiago was an influential businessman in San Diego and the father of Maria Clara. Betrothing his daughter to Crisostomo Ibarra, Capitan Tiago struggled to obey the will of the friars.
